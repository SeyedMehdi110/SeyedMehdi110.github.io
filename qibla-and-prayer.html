Qibla & Prayer

A practical companion for Qibla direction and daily prayer times, published by Larki Apps.

Overview

Qibla & Prayer is designed for straightforward everyday use. It helps you find the Qibla direction and view daily prayer times using configurable calculation methods. The app is offered as a free experience without advertising and does not require a user account.

Features

  • Qibla direction
  • Daily prayer times
  • Configurable prayer calculation methods
  • Prayer notifications
  • Location-based calculations
  • Manual location selection
  • A free experience without advertising
  • No user account requirement
  • An optional external PayPal link to support the developer, which does not unlock features or provide digital benefits

Optional developer support

The app may include an optional external PayPal link so users can support the developer if they wish. Any such payment is voluntary, is processed by PayPal under its own terms and privacy policy, and does not unlock features or provide digital benefits within the application.

Important disclaimer

Prayer times and Qibla direction may depend on the selected calculation method, location settings, device sensors and environmental conditions. Users should verify important religious timing requirements using a trusted local source.
